About Me

I am Md Ariful Islam, a data-driven analyst with a strong foundation in statistics, data science, and machine learning. I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Statistics and Analytics at the University of Arkansas, building upon a robust academic and professional background that includes my role as a Statistical Officer at the Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics (BBS).

My technical skills span R, Python, SQL, and R Shiny, with hands-on experience in statistical modeling, Bayesian inference, spatial and time series analysis, and dashboard development. I have contributed to over a dozen national-level surveys and projects involving complex data collection, analysis, and reporting—particularly in public health, socio-economic indicators, and financial systems.

I enjoy transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. Whether it’s predicting economic trends, developing real-time dashboards, or building machine learning models to support decision-making, I bring both rigor and creativity to solving data problems that matter.
